What I think about school

Language


Language overall


I think that it is very important to learn another language. Learning another language is an open door to millions of possibilities. When you learn another language, you can communicate with many more people than you could if you only spoke one. Overall, learning a language in school is great.





What I like about language classes


I love learning another language (spanish). It allows me to connect with more people, and it allows me to learn about the culture of the people whose language I am learning. I also really like the teacher I have this year, so my experience with language is a positive one.





What I don't like about language classes


There isn't really anything I don't like about language classes, other than the fact that some people get placed in the wrong level, since sometimes there is no test to place higher.
