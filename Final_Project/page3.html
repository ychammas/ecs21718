What I think about school

English


English overall


English is a great subject, because it is important to know how to read. It also helps you learn to analyze things, since it is a large portion of study in english. Also, in some schools, you are required to do a service learning project, and write an essay about it. This is good because it teaches students the importance of helping the community. Overall english is good, but I personally don't enjoy all of the aspects.





What I like about english


I enjoy writing essays because it allows me to express my ideas about any subject, in a professional way. This is something I like because I have many opinions, especially in regards to social justice, and politics. I also enjoy when we read books as a class, since I am able to talk to my classmates about the content.





What I don't like about english


I personally dislike being told what to read and when, so if we are reading something in class that is boring, I am more likely to procrastinate and not read. I also don't like how there are so many terms and vocabulary that I have to memorize. I like that it allows us to expand our vocabulary, but I really don't understand why I have to memorize words that are outdated, no longer used, or so obsolete that I would never use it in my daily life.
