Yasmina Chammas

About Me

My name is Yasmina and I am a student at Lane Tech. I was on the freshman basketball team, and we won the city championship 2018. I am also a semester one fresman Lane scholar. I like to play basketball and soccer in my free time, and I like to play Mario Kart.

Why I wanted to make this

I wanted to make this website because I have many opinions about school, and different classes I am taking in school. Like most students, I have my favourite and least favourite classes, and I wanted to have a place that I can describe how I feel and what I think.




Site Overview


This site is a place for me to express what I feel about subjects that I learn while in school. Each link at the top will take you to a page about that specific subject. In each individual page I will describe how I feel when I am in that class, and what I think about the subject as a whole.





School as a whole


School as a whole is pretty good. I think it is great that people can get a good education for free, if they are in a public school system. School is also pretty great socially, because you can make friends, and thats cool. Sometimes the social aspect causes problems, but I guess thats just life. Some people have different methods of learning, so I wish all schools could have the capacity to be able to care for everyone, this way, no one will have to travel super far to go to a school they can actually learn at. I also wish that people in poorer countries had better access to education. Overall school is pretty good, but it would be better if they could feel safe coming to school, but thats a topic for another day, and another site.
