What I think about school

Sciences


Science overall


In the past I have had bad experiences with science teachers. Though I enjoy learning about the science behind everyday things, the teachers I had ruined the experince for me. If I would have had different teachers, I feel as though I would have a more positive experience with science, and, in turn, would enjoy the subject more.





What I like about science


I enjoy learning about chemistry, and anything thay has to do with the periodic table. I also enjoy learning about the environment. Other than that, there isn't much I like about science classes.





What I don't like about science


There are lots of things that I don't like about science class. First, I do not like the repetition that occurs. I am currently learning the same thing I learned in 3rd and 4th grade, and I am in 9th grade. I would like it better if instead of repetition, they would expand on things we already know, going further in depth into topics. I also do not like learning about the scientific method. It's just really boring to me.
