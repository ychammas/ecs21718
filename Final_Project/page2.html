What I think about school

Mathematics


Mathematics overall


Personally, I really enjoy math. I really like to solve problems, and thats what math is. I am currently in H Geometry, and my favourite parts of the class are when we get to solve things algebraically. Sometimes the math can get hard, but the obstacles are easy to overcome. Overall, math is my favourite subject. Knowing how to do simple math is important, since you never know when you might need to use it.





What I like about math


So as I said before, I like to solve problems algebraically. Solving problems comes easy to me, as well as new skills in math. It's obvious that I would like to do something that I can learn easily, which is why I like math. It's also can be fun, depending on what type of math you are doing.





What I don't like about math


I am not so good at mental math, so doing any math without a calculator is not fun. I also don't like word problems that make absolutely no sense, like "Johnny buys 49 watermelons for $1 each". I would rather do a word problem that actually makes sense.
