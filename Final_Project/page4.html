What I think about school

History


History overall


History classes are generally pretty boring, but they are important because you need to know about things that happenend in the past. If we didn't know about things that happened in the past, we would have no idea why things are the way they are, which is something we should know.





What I like about history


I generally enjoy learning about things that happened in the past. It is pretty cool to see how people used to live, and what the world used to be like. I also like to be able to know about the history of other locations, not just the USA. I also enjoy the interactive activities that connect to what is being taught. History is a decent subject.





What I don't like about history


History classes tend to be very boring and textbook heavy, which are two things I don't like. Retaining information from a textbook is boring, and sometimes harder than retaining it from an activity or powerpoint. If there was a way to make history classes less boring, it would be a fun subject. Another thing I don't like is the fact that the American school system is biased, based off of themselves. Bits and pieces are changed so it fits the liking of "the big guys", and to make the US seem like the best of all. I wish that history was not biased like this.
